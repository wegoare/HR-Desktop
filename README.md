# HR-Desktop
Summary

The HR Analytics Dashboard provides a centralized solution for analyzing workforce data through interactive visualizations and detailed employee records. It helps HR managers monitor employee trends, demographics, salary insights, and organizational performance for better decision-making.

Requirements
Track hired, active, and terminated employees
Analyze workforce demographics and education distribution
Monitor salary patterns across departments and education levels
Compare HQ and branch employee distribution
Provide detailed employee-level records with filtering capabilities
Enable interactive dashboard exploration through slicers and visuals
Key KPIs
Total Employees
Active Employees
Terminated Employees
Attrition Rate
Average Salary
Gender Ratio
Department-wise Headcount
Education Distribution
Salary by Education Level
Business Use Cases
Workforce planning and resource allocation
Employee attrition and hiring analysis
Diversity and demographic monitoring
Salary benchmarking and compensation analysis
Department performance and staffing insights
Data-driven HR decision making
